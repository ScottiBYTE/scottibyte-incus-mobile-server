Initial Admin Setup

Create the first local admin account. Credentials and 2FA secret are stored in SQLite, not .env.

2FA Setup

Your admin account was created. Now add 2FA to your authenticator app.

  1. Open your authenticator app.
  2. Choose Add account or Scan QR code.
  3. Scan the QR code below.
  4. If scanning does not work, manually enter the Manual Secret.
  5. Save the Manual Secret in your password manager. It will not be shown again.
Advanced: otpauth URL

The otpauth URL is what the QR code contains. Most users do not need to copy it manually.

Do not continue until the account is added to your authenticator app.
